What we do

Some services we provide for our children

Here are some of the services we provide for the children under our care, ensuring they receive comprehensive support tailored to their individual needs:

  • Family support

    We offer counseling, workshops, and support groups to empower parents and caregivers with the knowledge and resources they need to advocate for their child's well-being.

  • Health benefits

    We provide access to comprehensive health benefits, including medical care, mental health services, nutritional support and therapeutic services. Inclusive children have all what they need.

  • Scholarships

    The cost of specialized education and therapy services can be a barrier for many families. That's why we offer scholarships and financial assistance programs to ensure that every child has access to the care they need, regardless of their financial circumstances.

  • Therapy

    Therapy for children with special needs is personalized to empower them, addressing their unique challenges and promoting holistic development for enhanced quality of life.
